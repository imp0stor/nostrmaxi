## FAQ

### What is NIP-05?

NIP-05 is Nostr's verification standard. It links your Nostr pubkey to a human-readable identifier (like `name@domain.com`), making you easier to find and trust.

Think of it like Twitter's blue check, but decentralized and actually meaningful.

### Why NostrMaxi vs other NIP-05 providers?

Most NIP-05 services sell you an identity and stop there. We're building a full creator platform ‚Äî feeds, profiles, publishing tools, e-commerce.

Plus, we offer multiple domains (nostrmaxi.com, strangesignal.ai, strange.news) and BYOD support.

### Can I bring my own domain?

Yes! Creator and Studio tiers support custom domains. You'll get DNS instructions and verification support.

### Do I own my identity if I leave?

Your Nostr identity (npub) is always yours. The NIP-05 identifier is tied to the domain. If you bring your own domain, you control it forever. If you use ours and leave, the identifier stops working, but your npub + content remain yours.

### What payment methods do you accept?

Bitcoin (Lightning or on-chain) via BTCPay. Credit cards coming soon.

### Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. No contracts, no tricks. Cancel from your dashboard whenever you want.
